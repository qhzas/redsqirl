Splitter

Red Sqirl is split into three sections (left, top right, top left). The size of each one can be modified at will with the vertical and horizontal splitters.

Vertical Splitter

When clicking right on the mouse, a menu appears with the three options for Right, Middle, and Left. Clicking on the splitter option changes the position to Left, Middle or Right.

  • Right - The splitter moves to the right
  • Middle - The splitter moves to the middle
  • Left - The splitter moves to the left

Horizontal Slitter

When clicking right on the mouse, a menu appears with the three options for Top, Middle, and Bottom. Clicking on the splitter option changes the position to Top, Middle or Bottom.

  • Top - The splitter moves to the top
  • Middle - The splitter moves to the middle
  • Bottom - The splitter moves to the bottom
