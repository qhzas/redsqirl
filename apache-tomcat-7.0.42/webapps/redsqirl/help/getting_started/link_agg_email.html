Link the Spark aggregator with the email node

The send email action is quite useful to produce a notification when an intermediate step is reached.

Let's link the Spark Aggregator to the Send Email action. A notification will be sent after the aggregator action finishing running. As before you can click in the link area between the action and the arc. Here we are suggesting another method to obtain the same result

  1. Hover over the node.
  2. Press Ctrl.
  3. Click options.
  4. Click on create link.
