Open output of the Spark aggregator

Once the workflow has successfully completed (you should have received an email), the next natural thing to do is to display the output.

  1. Hover over the Spark Aggregator action.
  2. Press ctrl.
  3. Click Options > Data Output. The data output will open after some time. It will also show a partial result.
  4. Click OK to close the window.
