Open HDFS view

We would like to investigate why Spark is taking so long to display the output and shows only a partial result. Red Sqirl is great for building analytic workflows, but it also brings Hadoop ecosystem together in one place. Let's open a Hadoop file system view and check our Spark Aggregator data manually.

  1. Above the help, you will notice if you hover over it with your mouse a horizontal bar. Drag this bar to about half of your screen height.
  2. You will notice a white area with a red triangle in the top left corner. Click on this triangle and choose Hadoop file system.
