Red Sqirl expects that the number of fields for every line should be the same. In this case Red Sqirl does not find the right delimiter so we must indicate it. Also, we will take a moment to assign meaningful names to the fields.

  1. Here, we would like to split by "=", replace #1 by "=" next to the delimiter property.
  2. Click Apply.
  3. Red Sqirl will warn you that the number of fields changes. Click OK
  4. Click the little pencil icon, next to Field1.
  5. Change the field names to "PROP_NAME" and "PROP_VALUE".
  6. Click OK.
  7. On the success message, click OK.
  8. Click OK, to exit from the Source action.
